Modelling and Control of Dynamical Systems.

My main area of interest is the study of dynamical systems with a special focus in their mathematical description, and how to use it to control such systems.

Furthermore, I am interested in developing robust and reliable modeling techniques. In the same manner, in construct Process-Driven models and Data-Driven models, applying to the latter data science techniques. Moreover, I am curious about how the underlying structures of dynamical systems can improve the generation of more accurate Data-Driven models.
